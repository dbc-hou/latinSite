LATIN FOR THE END OF THE WORLD

Grammar—Cases and Functions

In this volume we added three new functions for nouns to the 18 or so introduced previously. This time, instead of simply matching functions to cases or endings as in Volumes I and II, match the function with the underlined portion of a sentence that exemplifies that function. The matching is one-to-one.
