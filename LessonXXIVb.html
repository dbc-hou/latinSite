Vocabulary—Verbs (Infinitive Forms)

For each verb give the following:

  • the form in the person, number, tense, and voice requested (indicative only, no need for imperative forms yet)
  • an English meaning of that form
  • whichever of the following is appropriate:
    • D for verbs that take Dative objects
    • I for intransitive verbs
    • T for transitive verbs
    • I + T for verbs that can be either intransitive or transitive
